Maven Constructions Limited

Maven Constructions Limited is a trusted name in professional waterproofing and sealant services. With extensive industry experience, we specialize in protecting residential and commercial spaces from water damage, leakage, and structural deterioration.

Our focus is simple — deliver durable, high-performance solutions that enhance the longevity, safety, and value of every property we work on.

Our Core Services

We provide precision-based sealing and waterproofing solutions across a wide range of applications:

Windows & Doors

Professional gap sealing to prevent water intrusion, air leakage, and long-term structural damage.

Movement Joints

Flexible and durable sealing systems designed to accommodate structural expansion and contraction.

Wardrobes & Interior Finishes

Precise sealing for dust control, moisture resistance, and refined interior detailing.

Kitchen Areas

Moisture-resistant sealants that maintain hygiene, safety, and durability in high-use environments.

Glass-to-Glass Sealing

Clean, clear, and seamless proofing for glass joints with superior finishing standards.

Bathrooms & Wet Areas

Long-lasting waterproof protection to prevent seepage and mold formation.
